Autoindigestion

Autoindigestion is installed and will automatically run every morning at 8:30 AM and every evening at 8:30 PM. iTunes Connect reports are usually available by 8:00 AM Cupertino time (UTC -0800 or -0700 in summer) but are occasionally delayed a few hours. To change this schedule, edit /Library/LaunchDaemons/Autoindigestion.plist then reload it using launchctl.

Important! In order to download iTunes Connect reports, you must add one or more vendor plist files to /Library/Autoindigestion/Vendors. You can use the ExampleVendor.plist as a guide.

iTunes Connect reports will be downloaded to /Users/Shared/iTunes Connect.

For detailed information about Autoindigestion configuration options, visit the Autoindigestion project site
